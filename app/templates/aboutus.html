Our Mission

The visual media that we consume as a society is indoctrinated with harmful stereotypes concerning marginalized groups, specifically people of color, members of the LGBTQ+ community, and those subjected to gendered social norms. This provides a false and detrimental model for reality, pushing forth various unreasonable and unrealistic expectations and social standards for members of these demographics.

filmScale aims to provide transparency and statistics regarding any harmful stereotypes and/or themes that appear in the movies that we consume, as well as creating communication between users to share reviews, opinions, and criticisms on certain films regarding its themes and messages.
